Find Leads

Source prospects from LinkedIn — signal-based or search-based

Signal-Based Sourcing

Paste a LinkedIn post URL — your own post, a competitor's, or an industry post. The system finds people who liked or commented on it. Engaging with relevant content is one of the strongest "in-market" signals, and these leads get a scoring bonus automatically.

Search-Based Sourcing

Paste a LinkedIn Sales Navigator or People Search URL with your filters already applied (role, industry, location). The system scrapes, deduplicates, scores against your ICP, and queues anything that qualifies.

How scoring works

Every sourced lead is scored 0–100 against the ICP in config/icp.json: role fit, industry fit, company size, geography, and intent signals. Leads sourced from post engagement get an automatic signal bonus, since active engagement with relevant content is a stronger buying signal than a cold search match. Only leads scoring above the brand's threshold (default 60) are queued for outreach — everything else is logged but not contacted. No AI is used in scoring; it's all rule-based against config/icp.json and modules/prospecting/signals.js.
